Research

I am currently working on statistical inference methods for curved shapes embedded in n-dimensional vector space. The goal of the research project is to exploit the inherently non-linear geometery of shape space to learn shape distributions, predict lables, and defined a reasonable metric. Apart from image processing and understanding problems, I have a strong interest in machine learning problems. Particularly, in the follwoing directions

  • Graphical models and neural networks
  • Manifold and metric learning
  • Learning theory
