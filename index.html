Girum Getachew Demisse

I am a Phd student working in statistical shape modelling at University of Luxembourg. Prior to that, I got my MSc from Jacobs University Bremen and BSc from Hawassa University.

You can reach me at girum.demisse@uni.lu or girumdemisse@gmail.com

Research Interest

Currently, I am actively working on representation and modelling of curved shapes/surfaces embedded in n-dimensional vector space. However, I have a general and strong interest in machine learning, mathematical modelling and in geometry and invariance.
